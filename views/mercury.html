A little bit about Mercury

Mercury is the first planet in the solar system, as it is the closest to our sun. Being only slightly smaller than Earth's moon, Mercury is the smallest
in our solar system. (After the reclassification of Pluto to a dwarf moon, of course.) In 2004, NASA launched the "Messenger" probe satellite. It took 7 years
to reach Mercury and has been orbiting the planet since.
