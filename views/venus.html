A little bit about Venus

Venus is the second closest planet to the sun, and is the closest in size to Earth. Because of this, its sometimes called
Earth's twin. The conditions on Venus are often reffered to as "hellish". Because of a thick, dense atmosphere of carbon dioxide and clouds of sulphur
very little heat escapes from the surface of Venus. Temperatures on Venus rise as high as 870° F.
