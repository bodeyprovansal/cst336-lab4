A little bit about Earth

Earth is the 3rd planet from the sun, and has the most unique characteristics out of any planet in our solar system. As the only
planet with a rich oxygen atmosphere and large surface oceans of water, Earth has the only hosbitable environment that is able to sustain life.
Because it is our home planet, we use its measurements as standards to compare all other planets. Our days are measured by its time to complete one rotation
around its axis and our years are as long as it takes for Earth to complete one revolution around the sun.
